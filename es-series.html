EXTRA SLIM SUBWOOFER

Extra slim subwoofer

You can with just a regular set of speakers. You don’t, strictly speaking, need a subwoofer. But you cannot believe the difference having one will make. This speaker box of wonder will do incredible things to your sound system. It’s not just the inevitable boost to the low end that comes from a good quality subwoofer; it’s the overall richness of the sound quality, the sense of fullness that comes when you plug it in and turns it on. It’s fantastic for the whole sound system。

Application

Extra slim subwoofer is suitable for small area system, Such as Bars, Restaurants, Shopping malls, Small mobile system, Private party, KTV rooms
